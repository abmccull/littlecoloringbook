# Funnel Copy Refresh

Date: 2026-04-10
Campaign: `funnel-copy-refresh`

## Core Angle

`Screen-Free Mom Win`

Turn the favorite photos already on a parent's phone into a screen-free activity a child will want to use now and a keepsake the family will want to keep later.

## Support Angles

- `Personalized Without Becoming A Project`
- `Giftable Keepsake From The Camera Roll`

## Voice Rules

- Warm, bright, parent-real
- Short-to-medium sentences
- Outcome first, convenience second, proof third
- Never explain the product like an internal tool
- Keep the copy mobile-scannable

## Offer Ladder

- `10 pages`: mini starter after the free sample
- `30 pages`: good starter
- `50 pages`: better choice
- `100 pages`: best-value keepsake

## Format Framing

- `Print Tonight PDF`
- `Giftable Spiral Book + PDF`

## Funnel Jobs

### Homepage

- Job: sell the free sample first
- Main promise: easy screen-free win from the camera roll
- Primary CTA: `Get My Free Sample Page`
- Secondary CTA: `Build My Book`

### Free Sample Page

- Job: get the email submit
- Main promise: one favorite photo is enough to see if this feels like your family
- Primary CTA: start the free sample

### Sample Processing

- Job: keep the user calm and moving
- Main promise: we are turning their photo into a real preview
- Copy rule: no manual-dashboard language

### Sample Ready

- Job: convert the sample into the paid path
- Main promise: if the sample lands, the full book is the obvious next step
- Primary CTA: `Build The 100-Page Book`
- Secondary CTA: `Get The Spiral Book Version`
- Downsell CTA: `Keep It Light With 10 Pages`

### Builder

- Job: guide the user through a clean good-better-best decision
- Main promise: choose the version that fits how full the camera roll already is
- Copy rule: 30 stays visible, 50 feels better, 100 is best value

### Uploads

- Job: make photo upload feel like progress, not admin work
- Main promise: this is where the book becomes real

### Shipping

- Job: make delivery feel simple and gift-ready
- Main promise: pick the shipping speed that fits, with the PDF still included

### Order Confirmation + Portal

- Job: reassure, track progress, and open the next upsell
- Main promise: we have it from here, and the order page is the home base

## Copy Decisions Locked

- The product is not sold as `AI image generation`
- `Giftable`, `screen-free`, `camera roll`, `favorite photo`, and `keepsake` stay in regular rotation
- Error and fallback messages should sound human, not operational

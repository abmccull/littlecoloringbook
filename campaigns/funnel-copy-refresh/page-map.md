- Mid-page CTA: free sample before commitment

- Promise: turn one photo they already love into a free coloring page
- Reassurance: one photo is enough
- Desired emotion: low-risk, easy, worth trying tonight

## Sample Processing

- Promise: your free page is on the way
- Reassurance: we keep checking for you
- Desired emotion: guided, calm, no-refresh-needed

## Sample Ready

- Promise: add the photos that tell this version of the story
- Reassurance: a mix of close-ups, pets, siblings, trips, and everyday moments works well

## Shipping

- Promise: choose where we should send the spiral book
- Reassurance: the PDF is still included
- Desired emotion: simple, gift-ready, clear on timing

## Confirmation

- Promise: you are officially in
- Reassurance: the order page is where everything updates
- Desired emotion: relief and anticipation

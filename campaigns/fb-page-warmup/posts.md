# Facebook Page Warm-Up — 14-Day Seed Plan

**Page:** little color book (FB)
**Cross-post to:** @littlecolorbook (IG, after rename is complete)
**Purpose:** Seed the blank Page with organic posts before running paid ads. Meta's risk-score system penalizes ad accounts launching paid creative against an empty Page — we need at least 10 posts across ≥14 days before the first paid campaign goes live.
**Author:** AI-drafted 2026-04-17, ready for Alec to review, tweak, and schedule manually via the Page's native scheduler.
**Voice reference:** `brand/voice-profile.md` (Screen-Free Mom Win). Post 1 is in Alec's personal voice; the rest are brand voice.

**Cadence rationale:** 10 posts across 14 days averages ~0.7/day. Not every day has a post — that looks more natural than daily cadence on a brand-new account. Saturdays and Tuesdays lean best for engagement in this audience; rest days (4, 7, 9, 11) keep it from feeling mechanical.

**First-comment pattern on IG:** Always drop the link in the first comment (since IG captions don't support clickable links). The caption itself can say "Link in bio" or no URL call-out.

## Production notes for Alec

**Image sourcing:** Use `scripts/pick-warmup-images.ts` (to be built in Phase 2) to surface the top candidates from the 1,300 test batch, or manually pick the most polished 10 for now. Criteria: clean subject, no text overlays, good white space, recognizable kid-friendly content.

**Cropping for Meta:** FB feed prefers 1:1; IG feed prefers 4:5; Stories 9:16. Prepare each post image in 1:1 + 4:5 at minimum. Reels are out of scope for warm-up.

**Hashtags (IG only, keep light — 3–5 per post):** `#coloringbook #kidactivities #screenfree #personalizedgift #rainydayactivities` — rotate, don't reuse the same set.

**Do NOT:**
- Post real photos of real children without written consent, even your own kid if you plan to run this as ad creative later. Use illustrated/finished-page mockups only during warm-up.
- Use "before/after" child imagery (Meta policy).
- Use countdown timers, urgency language, or "claim yours" scarcity in any warm-up post.
- Link out more than once per week — the crawler notices heavy link posting on new Pages.

**After posting:**
- Reply to every comment for the first 14 days, fast. Organic engagement on warm-up posts is the single biggest risk-score input Meta sees.
- Like a handful of posts from adjacent parenting accounts — standard human-looking activity.

**Scheduling:** The Page's native Meta Business Suite scheduler handles all of this without the automation stack. Schedule all 10 in one sitting today.

# Email 2: Seasonal Gift Hook

## Subject Line Variants

### A: "Birthdays, holidays, and the last-minute gift move" — recommended
Three concrete gifting moments. Hooks on the "last-minute" reality most parents and gift-givers face. 50 chars — right at the edge.

### B: "A gift hiding in your camera roll"
Curiosity + outcome. Leans on the core positioning ("camera roll"). Slightly bolder, opens a mental loop.

### C: "a giftable book in about 15 minutes"
Lowercase, utility-first, specific time commitment. Low-pressure but high-signal for moms who need "tell me how long."

**Recommended A/B test:** A vs B
**Reason:** Tests multiple-occasion framing vs single-curiosity frame. Winner reveals whether re-engagement segment responds more to "a reason" or "a question."

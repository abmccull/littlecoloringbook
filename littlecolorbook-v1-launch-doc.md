# littlecolorbook.com V1 Launch Doc

## Working Brand

**Brand name:** littlecolorbook.com

**Tagline:** Turn favorite family photos into coloring books your kids can color.

**One-line pitch:** littlecolorbook.com turns real family photos into personalized coloring books for kids, delivered as a print-at-home PDF or a spiral-bound book shipped to your door.

## Positioning

This is not an AI art tool. It is a personalized memory product for parents and gift buyers who want a meaningful, screen-free activity for kids.

### Core angle

Turn real family memories into something a child can hold, color, and keep.

### Primary buyer

Moms ages 28-42 with kids roughly ages 3-8.

### Secondary buyer

Grandparents, aunts, and gift buyers who are more likely to choose the printed version.

### Why it wins

- It is personalized.
- It feels emotional, not generic.
- It is easy to buy.
- It is screen-free.
- It doubles as both an activity and a keepsake.

## Recommended Landing Page Flow

### Cold traffic page

Drive paid and organic traffic to a simple page with:

- before/after photo-to-page examples
- free sample CTA
- one-photo upload
- email capture

Do not ask cold traffic to upload 10 photos immediately.

### Sample delivery page

After they submit:

- show "your sample is being created"
- collect child first name if not already captured
- show upgrade options while they wait

### Sample email

Subject line: Your free coloring page is ready

Body:

- preview image
- CTA to turn this into a full book
- lead with the 30-design offer

## Funnel

### Funnel structure

1. Organic post, ad, creator mention, or Pinterest pin
2. Landing page with free sample offer
3. Upload 1 photo and enter email
4. Deliver free sample page
5. Present core paid offer with 30 designs as default
6. Offer one-click upgrade to printed spiral book
7. Post-purchase upsell for extra copies or seasonal add-on
8. Follow-up emails for non-buyers and reorder emails for buyers

### Best checkout logic

- Default the customer to 30 designs
- Let them choose PDF or Print + PDF
- Offer 50 and 100 as clear value upgrades
- After PDF purchase, offer "Add a spiral-bound printed copy"
- After print purchase, offer "Add an extra copy for grandparents"

### Post-purchase upsells

- Extra printed copy
- Grandparent gift copy
- Matching holiday pack
- Pet mini-book
- Sibling add-on

## Email Flow

### Free sample leads

**Email 1:** deliver sample and offer the 30-design book

**Email 2:** show 3 example books and remind them the printed version includes the PDF

**Email 3:** position it as a screen-free activity for rainy days, travel, and quiet time

**Email 4:** position it as a meaningful gift for grandparents or birthdays

**Email 5:** final reminder with strongest before/after examples

### Buyers

**Email 1:** order confirmation and timing expectations

**Email 2:** tips for printing or what to expect from the spiral version

**Email 3:** ask for a photo of the child coloring the pages

**Email 4:** offer an extra copy or a second themed book

**Email 5:** seasonal reorder prompt

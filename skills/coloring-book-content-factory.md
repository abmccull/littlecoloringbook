# Coloring Book Content Factory

## Purpose

Use this skill for daily or scheduled work related to:

- short-form content production
- paid creative testing
- creator and affiliate content adaptation
- occasion campaign generation
- weekly performance synthesis

## Inputs

Read these first:

- `AGENTS.md`
- `HEARTBEAT.md`
- `campaign-taxonomy.yaml`
- latest performance exports
- latest experiment registry
- latest approved asset folders

## Steps

1. Read current winners, losers, campaign calendar, and approved offers.
2. Ingest latest platform and landing-page performance metrics.
3. Rank creative archetypes by weighted score.
4. Select exploit, adjacent, and explore batches.
5. Write scripts, overlays, and thumbnail text.
6. Generate product-truth visuals from internal tools.
7. Generate avatar variants in Arcads where appropriate.
8. Generate or dub voiceovers in ElevenLabs where appropriate.
9. Assemble assets, captions, metadata, and filenames.
10. Queue for publishing or paid testing.
11. Save learning notes and propose the next batch.

## Hard Rules

- Do not publish without product proof or a strong approved reason.
- Do not fabricate customer stories.
- Do not create fake child-generated or parent-generated footage without clear internal labeling.
- Keep every asset tied to a specific hypothesis.
- Preserve the metadata taxonomy for every asset.
- Prefer descendants of winners over random novelty.

## Experiment Logic

### Exploit

Use for proven hook and angle families.

### Adjacent

Use for near-winners where one variable change may unlock performance.

### Explore

Use for genuinely new angles, new occasion frames, or new format families.

## Output Contract

At the end of each run, write:

- `ops-summary.md`
- `today-asset-batch.csv` or `today-asset-batch.json`
- `paid-nominations.md`
- `next-experiments.md`

Each output must identify:

- top winners
- weakest assets
- what was published
- what was queued
- what should be produced next

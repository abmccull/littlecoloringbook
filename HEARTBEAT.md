# HEARTBEAT.md

## Operating Rhythm

This file defines the cadence for the OpenClaw content operator.

### Daily

1. Ingest yesterday's platform and landing-page data.
2. Refresh winner and loser status.
3. Select exploit, adjacent, and explore batches.
4. Generate scripts and creative assets.
5. QA every approved asset.
6. Queue or publish organic content.
7. Nominate paid-ready content.
8. Log learnings and next steps.

### Weekly

1. Summarize best hooks, voices, personas, and occasions.
2. Identify creative fatigue.
3. Refresh creator briefs and affiliate kits.
4. Build one occasion-specific campaign pack.
5. Promote top organic winners into paid testing.

### Monthly

1. Produce a Gamma performance deck.
2. Update channel baselines.
3. Rank offers by contribution profit, not just CTR.
4. Rotate in next-priority occasion themes.
5. Review whether creator and affiliate share of acquisition is increasing.

## Weekly Priorities

The operator should prioritize in this order:

1. product-proof creatives
2. free sample conversion creatives
3. print and sibling bundle creatives
4. occasion campaign creatives
5. localization of winners

## Guardrails

- Do not sacrifice product truth for volume.
- Do not generate creative sprawl without tagged hypotheses.
- Do not scale paid without organic or conversion evidence.
- Do not add new personas, voices, or offers unless current tests justify expansion.

## Success Conditions

The system is improving if:

- free sample CPL is falling
- sample-to-purchase is rising
- print attach is rising
- sibling bundle attach is rising
- more content is coming from descendants of proven winners
- creator and affiliate sourced revenue is increasing
